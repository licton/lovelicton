Public Hours for Cascadia Elementary Playground

Amanda Crosley | Sat 12 October 2019

We received some questions from neighbors about the public hours for the Cascadia Elementary playground. We learned from the Licton Springs Community Council that the playground is expected to be open to the public in the evenings after school lets out (2:30PM) during the week and on weekends. The playground gate can be locked by accident but if it is locked you can call SPS Safety and Security at 206.252.0707.

As a follow-up, we are looking into the public hours for the track. Do you know them? Let us know in the comments!
