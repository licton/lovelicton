Food Trucks Come to Licton Springs!

Amanda Crosley | Sun 29 September 2019

In addition to getting your caffine fix at Pilgrim Coffee (located at Oak Tree Plaza) who have expanded their hours to 7am to 3pm, you can now enjoy lunch at food trucks located at North Seattle College from 11am - 2pm on weekdays. The food truck changes each day and you can find the menu here.
