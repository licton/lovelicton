Burke Night Out 2018!

Join your neighbors Tuesday, August 7th for Burke Night Out 2018

Join us for our yearly Block Party & Community Pot Luck 6-9 PM at the cul de sac at Burke Ave N and N 90th St. It's a great way to get to know your neighbors, discover and mobilize around areas of joint concern, and enjoy a Seattle summer night. This year, thanks to funding provided by Seattle's Neighborhood Matching Fund, we will have:

  • Proteins catered by Otto's BBQ
  • Non-Alcoholic beverages provided for all ages
  • Bubble Makers and Bubble Wands
  • Scavenger Hunt
  • Snow Cones
  • Face Painter
  • Entertainer

No need to bring anything, but sides, desserts, chairs, and/or any donations for the food drive will be appreciated.

Hope to see you there!
