Keep Licton Local: Support Community Business!

Have fun, explore new places, help local businesses thrive, and win great prizes! It's as easy as:

Shop at any of the local businesses on the map below:
Take a picture of your receipt with your phone.
Email the receipt to lovelicton@gmail.com.

Repeat for every trip to a local business with each receipt representing an additional entry. Every month we randomly pick one or more entries to receive a prize! We will respond to your email if you are a winner for further instructions.
